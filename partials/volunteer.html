Volunteer

All Saints Literacy Center is seeking volunteer tutors to help adults learn to read, write and speak in English. Tutors work one to one with English as a Second Language (ESL) learners that need help learning English to meet their goals. All training and materials are provided. It only takes two hours a week to make a difference in someone’s life!

>

Tutor qualifications:

  • 18 years or older
  • Comfortable reading and writing in English
  • Have a positive attitude
  • Be patient and encouraging
  • Empathetic to second language learners’ needs
  • Be flexible and willing to learn strategies to help a person succeed
  • No previous teaching or tutoring experiences is needed
  • High school graduate and completion of some college coursework
  • Able to commit to 6 months of service
  • Willing to learn computer software that learners will use
  • No previous computer knowledge is needed, just a willingness to learn!
